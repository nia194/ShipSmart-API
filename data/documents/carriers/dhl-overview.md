# DHL Express — Carrier Overview

## Company Profile
DHL is the world's leading international logistics company, part of Deutsche Post DHL Group. DHL Express operates in over 220 countries and territories. DHL's primary strength is international express delivery, though it also offers domestic services in select markets.

## Service Levels

### DHL Express Worldwide
- **Transit time**: 2-5 business days internationally
- **Best for**: International shipments requiring speed and reliability
- **Coverage**: 220+ countries
- **Tracking**: Door-to-door tracking included

### DHL Express 12:00
- **Transit time**: Next possible business day by noon
- **Best for**: Time-critical international shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered before 12:00 PM

### DHL Express 9:00
- **Transit time**: Next possible business day by 9:00 AM
- **Best for**: Most urgent international shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered before 9:00 AM

### DHL Domestic Express
- **Transit time**: 1-2 business days
- **Best for**: Domestic shipments in markets where DHL has domestic service
- **Note**: Domestic service availability varies by country. In the US, DHL domestic service is limited compared to UPS/FedEx.

## Pricing Factors
- Weight-based pricing with volumetric weight calculation
- DIM factor: 139 (same formula as domestic carriers)
- Country-pair routing (origin to destination country)
- Fuel surcharges and remote area surcharges
- Customs duties and import fees (for international)

## International Shipping Considerations
- Customs documentation required for most shipments
- Commercial invoice needed for goods with declared value
- Harmonized System (HS) codes required for customs classification
- Import duties and taxes are the responsibility of the receiver (unless DDP terms)
- DHL offers customs brokerage to expedite clearance

## Address Validation
DHL Express does not provide a standalone public address validation API. Address accuracy should be verified before shipping. DHL validates addresses during the shipment booking process internally.

## Key Strengths
- Largest international express delivery network
- Customs expertise and brokerage services
- Delivery to 220+ countries and territories
- Time-definite international express options
- DHL On Demand Delivery for recipient flexibility

## Limitations for US Users
- DHL's US domestic network is limited
- For US-to-US shipments, UPS/FedEx/USPS are typically better options
- DHL excels at US-to-international and international-to-US routes
- Pricing for domestic US may be higher than competitors

## Common Issues
- Customs delays can extend international delivery times
- Remote area surcharges for locations outside main service areas
- Duties and taxes at destination can surprise receivers
- Documentation errors can cause shipments to be held at customs
