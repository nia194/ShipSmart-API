# FedEx (Federal Express) — Carrier Overview

## Company Profile
FedEx is one of the world's largest express transportation companies, serving more than 220 countries and territories. Founded in 1971, FedEx pioneered overnight delivery and remains a leader in express shipping.

## Domestic Service Levels

### FedEx Ground
- **Transit time**: 1-5 business days
- **Best for**: Cost-effective, non-urgent shipments
- **Max weight**: 150 lbs
- **Note**: Operates Monday-Friday; does not deliver on weekends by default

### FedEx Home Delivery
- **Transit time**: 1-5 business days
- **Best for**: Residential addresses
- **Note**: Includes evening and weekend delivery options
- **Key difference**: Specifically designed for residential delivery

### FedEx Express Saver
- **Transit time**: 3 business days
- **Best for**: Economy express shipping
- **Guarantee**: Guaranteed 3rd business day delivery

### FedEx 2Day
- **Transit time**: 2 business days
- **Best for**: Time-sensitive at moderate cost
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by end of 2nd business day

### FedEx 2Day A.M.
- **Transit time**: 2 business days, morning delivery
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 10:30 AM on 2nd business day

### FedEx Standard Overnight
- **Transit time**: Next business day, afternoon
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 3:00 PM next business day (most areas)

### FedEx Priority Overnight
- **Transit time**: Next business day, morning
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 10:30 AM next business day

### FedEx First Overnight
- **Transit time**: Next business day, early morning
- **Best for**: Critical shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 8:00 AM next business day (select areas)

## Pricing Factors
- Billable weight: higher of actual weight or dimensional weight
- DIM factor: 139 (domestic)
- Zone-based pricing
- Surcharges: residential, oversized, fuel, delivery area surcharge
- Volume discounts with FedEx account

## Address Validation
FedEx provides an Address Validation API that:
- Resolves addresses to standardized format
- Classifies as residential or commercial
- Returns confidence score (RESOLVED, MIXED, UNRESOLVED)
- Provides attribute details

## Key Strengths
- Industry leader in overnight and express delivery
- FedEx SmartPost for lightweight residential packages (uses USPS for final mile)
- Strong international express network
- FedEx Delivery Manager for recipient control
- Comprehensive packaging supplies (free for Express shipments)

## Common Issues
- Home Delivery and Ground are separate networks (being merged)
- Delivery area surcharges for remote locations
- Signature requirements may cause missed deliveries
- Express services significantly more expensive than Ground
