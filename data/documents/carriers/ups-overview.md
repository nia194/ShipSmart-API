# UPS (United Parcel Service) — Carrier Overview

## Company Profile
UPS is the world's largest package delivery company, operating in over 220 countries. Founded in 1907, UPS handles approximately 25 million packages daily.

## Domestic Service Levels

### UPS Ground
- **Transit time**: 1-5 business days depending on distance
- **Best for**: Non-urgent shipments, cost-sensitive packages
- **Max weight**: 150 lbs
- **Tracking**: Full tracking included
- **Guarantee**: No guaranteed delivery date for Ground

### UPS 3 Day Select
- **Transit time**: 3 business days
- **Best for**: Moderately time-sensitive shipments
- **Max weight**: 150 lbs
- **Guarantee**: Guaranteed 3rd business day delivery

### UPS 2nd Day Air
- **Transit time**: 2 business days
- **Best for**: Time-sensitive shipments at moderate cost
- **Max weight**: 150 lbs
- **Guarantee**: Guaranteed 2nd business day delivery by end of day

### UPS 2nd Day Air A.M.
- **Transit time**: 2 business days, morning delivery
- **Best for**: Need-it-by-morning shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 10:30 AM or noon on 2nd business day

### UPS Next Day Air Saver
- **Transit time**: Next business day, afternoon delivery
- **Best for**: Urgent but not first-thing-in-morning
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by end of next business day

### UPS Next Day Air
- **Transit time**: Next business day
- **Best for**: Urgent shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 10:30 AM or noon next business day

### UPS Next Day Air Early
- **Transit time**: Next business day, earliest delivery
- **Best for**: Critical time-sensitive shipments
- **Guarantee**: Delivered by 8:00 AM next business day

## Pricing Factors
- Billable weight: higher of actual weight or dimensional weight
- DIM factor: 139 (domestic), 166 (international)
- Zone-based pricing (distance from origin to destination)
- Surcharges: residential delivery, oversized packages, fuel surcharge
- Discounts available with UPS account and volume shipping

## Address Validation
UPS provides an Address Validation API that checks:
- Whether an address is deliverable
- Whether the address is residential or commercial
- Normalized/standardized address format
- Suggestions for ambiguous addresses

## Key Strengths
- Extensive domestic network with reliable ground service
- Strong business-to-business delivery infrastructure
- Saturday delivery available for additional fee
- UPS My Choice for delivery management
- Integrated returns solutions

## Common Issues
- Residential surcharge adds $4-6 per package
- Large/oversized packages incur additional handling fees
- Delivery attempts limited to 3 before return
- Weekend delivery limited and costs extra
