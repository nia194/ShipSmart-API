# USPS (United States Postal Service) — Carrier Overview

## Company Profile
USPS is a government agency that provides postal service in the United States. It delivers to every address in the US, including PO boxes, military addresses (APO/FPO), and US territories. USPS handles approximately 129 billion pieces of mail and 7.3 billion packages annually.

## Domestic Service Levels

### USPS Ground Advantage
- **Transit time**: 2-5 business days
- **Best for**: Everyday shipping, packages up to 70 lbs
- **Max weight**: 70 lbs
- **Tracking**: Full tracking included
- **Note**: Replaced First-Class Package Service and Parcel Select Ground in 2023

### USPS Priority Mail
- **Transit time**: 1-3 business days
- **Best for**: Fast, affordable shipping with insurance included
- **Max weight**: 70 lbs
- **Insurance**: Up to $100 included
- **Note**: Free flat-rate boxes available; price based on weight and distance, or flat rate

### USPS Priority Mail Express
- **Transit time**: 1-2 days, guaranteed
- **Best for**: Urgent shipments with a guaranteed delivery date
- **Max weight**: 70 lbs
- **Insurance**: Up to $100 included
- **Guarantee**: Money-back guarantee if delivery is late
- **Note**: Includes Sunday and holiday delivery in most areas

### Media Mail
- **Transit time**: 2-8 business days
- **Best for**: Books, CDs, DVDs, and educational materials
- **Max weight**: 70 lbs
- **Restriction**: Content restrictions — only eligible media items

### USPS Retail Ground
- **Transit time**: 2-8 business days
- **Best for**: Large or heavy items not eligible for other services
- **Max weight**: 70 lbs

## Pricing Factors
- Weight-based pricing (max 70 lbs per package)
- DIM factor: 166 (more favorable than UPS/FedEx at 139)
- Zone-based pricing for most services
- Flat-rate options available for Priority Mail and Priority Mail Express
- No residential surcharge (unlike UPS and FedEx)
- Commercial pricing available through USPS business accounts

## Address Validation
USPS provides the most authoritative address validation for US addresses:
- Standardizes addresses to USPS format
- Validates deliverability
- Provides ZIP+4 codes
- Identifies address type

## Key Strengths
- Delivers to every US address including PO boxes and military APO/FPO
- No residential surcharge (significant savings for home delivery)
- Free package pickup from homes and businesses
- Free Priority Mail flat-rate boxes and envelopes
- Most affordable option for lightweight packages (under 1 lb)
- Saturday delivery included at no extra cost
- More favorable DIM factor (166 vs 139) for large, lightweight packages

## Limitations
- Maximum weight of 70 lbs (vs 150 lbs for UPS/FedEx)
- Maximum combined length + girth of 130 inches
- No guaranteed delivery time for Ground Advantage
- International services available but not as comprehensive as DHL
- Limited customer service compared to private carriers
- No evening or appointment delivery options

## Best Use Cases
- Lightweight packages (under 5 lbs): often cheapest option
- Residential deliveries: no surcharge advantage
- Flat-rate shipping: predictable costs regardless of weight
- PO Box and military address delivery: only option for some addresses
- High-volume small-parcel shipping with commercial rates
