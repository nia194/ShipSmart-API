# ShipSmart Shipping FAQ

## What carriers does ShipSmart support?

ShipSmart currently integrates with major shipping carriers including UPS, FedEx, USPS, and DHL. Each carrier offers different service levels ranging from economy ground shipping to express overnight delivery.

## How are shipping quotes calculated?

Shipping quotes are calculated based on package dimensions (length, width, height), weight, origin zip code, destination zip code, and selected service level. ShipSmart fetches real-time rates from each carrier's API and presents them side by side for easy comparison.

## What is dimensional weight?

Dimensional weight (DIM weight) is a pricing technique used by carriers. It calculates a theoretical weight based on a package's volume rather than its actual weight. The carrier charges whichever is higher: actual weight or dimensional weight. The formula is typically: (Length x Width x Height) / DIM factor. Common DIM factors are 139 for domestic and 166 for international shipments.

## Can I save shipping options for later?

Yes. ShipSmart allows you to save any quoted shipping option for later review. Saved options appear in your dashboard and remain available for 30 days. You can compare saved options side by side before making a final booking decision.

## How does booking work?

When you select a shipping option, ShipSmart redirects you to the carrier's booking page with your shipment details pre-filled. ShipSmart tracks the redirect for your records. The actual payment and label generation happen on the carrier's platform.

## What package types are supported?

ShipSmart supports standard boxes, padded envelopes, tubes, and custom dimensions. When requesting a quote, you can either select a predefined package type or enter custom dimensions. Weight limits vary by carrier and service level.

## Is there a maximum package size?

Maximum package dimensions depend on the carrier. Generally, most carriers accept packages up to 108 inches in length and 165 inches in combined length plus girth. Oversized packages may incur additional surcharges.
