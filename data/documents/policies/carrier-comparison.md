## When to Use Which Carrier

### Use UPS When:
- Shipping heavy packages (50-150 lbs)
- Need reliable ground service with consistent transit times
- Shipping to business addresses regularly
- Volume discounts are negotiated

### Use FedEx When:
- Express shipping is the primary need
- Shipping internationally with express service
- Need early-morning delivery guarantees
- Using FedEx packaging supplies (free for Express)

### Use USPS When:
- Package is lightweight (under 5 lbs)
- Delivering to residential addresses (no surcharge)
- Shipping to PO boxes or military addresses
- Need the cheapest option for small packages
- Using flat-rate boxes for dense, heavy items

### Use DHL When:
- Shipping internationally, especially to Europe/Asia
- Need customs brokerage and expertise
- Time-critical international delivery
- Shipping to countries where UPS/FedEx have limited coverage
