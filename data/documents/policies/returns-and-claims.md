# Returns and Claims Guide

## Filing a Damage Claim

### When to File
- Package arrived with visible external damage
- Contents are damaged or broken despite intact packaging
- Items are missing from the package
- Package arrived wet or water-damaged

### Claim Process by Carrier

**UPS Claims**
1. Go to ups.com/claims or call 1-800-742-5877
2. Provide tracking number and proof of value (receipt, invoice)
3. Provide photos of the damaged package and contents
4. Keep all original packaging — UPS may request inspection
5. Claims must be filed within 60 days of delivery
6. Processing time: 7-10 business days typically

**FedEx Claims**
1. Go to fedex.com/claimsportal or call 1-800-463-3339
2. Provide tracking number, proof of value, and damage photos
3. Keep original packaging for potential inspection
4. Claims must be filed within 60 days of delivery
5. Processing time: 5-7 business days typically

**USPS Claims**
1. Go to usps.com/insuranceclaims or visit local post office
2. Provide tracking number, proof of value
3. Proof of insurance (included with Priority Mail/Express)
4. Claims must be filed within 60 days
5. Processing time: 5-10 business days

**DHL Claims**
1. Contact DHL at 1-800-225-5345 or file online
2. Provide air waybill number and documentation
3. Timeframes vary by country and service
4. International claims may require customs documentation

### Tips for Successful Claims
- File as soon as possible — don't wait until near the deadline
- Take photos before and during unboxing
- Keep ALL packaging materials including inner cushioning
- Provide clear evidence of the item's value (purchase receipt, listing price)
- Be specific about what is damaged and estimated repair/replacement cost
- Respond promptly to carrier requests for additional information

## Lost Package Procedures

### When Is a Package Considered Lost?
- No tracking updates for 7+ business days (domestic)
- No tracking updates for 14+ business days (international)
- Tracking shows "delivered" but recipient never received it

### Steps to Take
1. **Verify the delivery address** is correct on the tracking page
2. **Check with neighbors** — packages may be left with nearby residents
3. **Check safe spots** — back door, garage, porch, with building manager
4. **Wait 24-48 hours** — sometimes "delivered" scans are premature
5. **File a trace/investigation** with the carrier
6. **File a claim** if investigation confirms the package is lost

### Carrier Contact for Lost Packages
- UPS: Request a "Package Trace" — 1-800-742-5877
- FedEx: Request a "Package Trace" — 1-800-463-3339
- USPS: Submit a "Missing Mail" search at usps.com
- DHL: Contact customer service for a trace investigation

## Return Shipping

### Carrier Return Solutions
- **UPS**: UPS Returns services — prepaid return labels, QR codes for label-free drops
- **FedEx**: FedEx Returns Technology — email return labels, print-at-home labels
- **USPS**: Return services via USPS.com, free package pickup for returns
- **DHL**: DHL Express return solutions for international returns

### Return Shipping Cost Tips
- Use USPS for returns under 5 lbs (typically cheapest)
- Provide prepaid return labels to improve customer experience
- Consider carrier-specific return programs for discounted rates
- For international returns, be aware of reverse customs requirements
